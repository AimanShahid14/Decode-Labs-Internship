Our story

About Bookhaven

Bookhaven opened its doors in 2015 with a simple idea: a bookstore should feel like a second home. What began as a single shelf of secondhand paperbacks has grown into a full community library and bookshop, welcoming readers of every age and every genre.

We believe that books deserve a slow, unhurried space — no bright lights, no rush to buy. Just quiet corners, good lighting, and shelves organized so you actually find what you didn't know you were looking for.
